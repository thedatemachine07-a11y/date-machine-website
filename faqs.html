What is the Date Machine?

Date Machine is a fun, creative vending machine service that delivers ready-to-go date kits. We make planning date night easy, exciting, and affordable!

Who is Date Machine for?

Anyone looking to spice up date night, try something new, or just have fun! Perfect for couples, friends, or first dates.

What kind of dates can I get from the machine?

Our vending machines offer unique, affordable, and easy-to-do date kits. Each kit includes everything you need for a fun experience — an activity, all the materials that you need for the activity (you never need to spend a dime anywhere else the date kits are all inclusive), get to know you/ icebreaker questions, and dinner for 2 or dessert for 2 already paid for!

How does the subscription work?

Subscribers get a weekly coupon code for a $25 Date Machine kit, delivered digitally. Each coupon is valid for one week. Think you will miss a week? Share the code with someone else — share the love!

How do I use a coupon from the machine or subscription?

Enter the coupon code in on the card reader at The Date Machine for a free 25 dollar date kit every single week!

Can I share a coupon with friends?

Yes! All coupons can be shared. We encourage you to share the love!

Where are your vending machines located?

Our Date Machine vending machine is located in the Women's Cedars Apartment Lobby in Hemming Village, Rexburg (155 W 2nd S, Rexburg, ID 83440) We will be expanding to Provo Utah this year so stay tuned!

Can I request a specific date kit or theme?

We love suggestions! Submit requests through our website’s contact form, and we’ll consider them for future kits!

How much does a kit cost?

Each kit ranges from $5-$35, the difference between the price of the kits depends on the value of the materials inside ( for example a 5 dollar date will have around 10 dollars worth of materials and dessert for 2 already paid for ) subscribers can get a free 25 dollar date kit every single week for only 46.99 a month!

Can I get my business in the Date Machine?

Yes! We partner with local businesses and event organizers. Contact us to coordinate a partnership!

Are the kits safe and family-friendly?

Absolutely. All kits are PG, safe, and fun.
